Roofing Types

Choose the right system

Every home and budget is different. We'll help you weigh durability, cost, and curb appeal across the options that perform best in Wisconsin.

Asphalt Shingles

The most popular choice in Madison — affordable, versatile, and available in architectural styles that mimic slate or wood. A great balance of value and longevity.

Metal Roofing

Standing-seam and metal panels that shrug off snow, resist fire, and last 50+ years. Energy-efficient and increasingly the premium choice for modern homes.

Flat & Low-Slope

TPO, EPDM, and modified-bitumen systems for porches, additions, and flat-roof homes — sealed tight against ponding water and ice.

Repairs & Replacements

Leaks, storm damage, missing shingles, or full re-roofs — we diagnose honestly and fix it right, whether that's a targeted repair or a complete replacement.
