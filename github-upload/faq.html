Roofing, Siding & Gutter Answers

Straight answers to the questions Madison homeowners ask us most — about costs, timelines, warranties, and how we work. Don't see your question? Give us a call.

Costs & Pricing

What will it cost?

How much does a new roof cost in Madison, WI?

Most asphalt shingle roof replacements in the Madison area run between roughly $8,000 and $20,000. Your number depends on the square footage and pitch of your roof, the materials you choose, the number of layers being torn off, and the condition of the wood decking underneath.

Smaller, simple roofs can come in lower, while large or steep homes and premium materials like standing-seam metal will run higher. Targeted roof repairs typically range from a few hundred dollars to a couple thousand. The best way to get an accurate figure for your home is a free, no-obligation estimate.

How much does new siding cost?

Siding replacement in the Madison area generally ranges from about $9,000 to $25,000 for an average single-family home. Vinyl is the most budget-friendly choice, fiber cement sits in the middle, and premium wood or specialty profiles cost more.

Final pricing depends on your home's square footage, the number of stories, the amount of trim and detail work, and whether any underlying sheathing or moisture damage needs repair. We provide itemized written quotes so you can see exactly where your investment goes.

How much do gutters cost?

Seamless gutter installation typically runs from about $1,200 to $4,000 for a full home in the Madison area, based on total linear footage, the number of downspouts, and the material and color you choose.

Gutter guards generally add about $5 to $12 per linear foot, and routine cleaning and maintenance visits are usually a few hundred dollars. Bundling gutters with a roof or siding project often makes the most of a single mobilization.

Project Timelines

How long does it take?

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in Madison are completed in one to three days once our crew begins, weather permitting. Larger homes or complex rooflines with multiple valleys, dormers, and steep pitches can take a bit longer.

We schedule carefully around Wisconsin weather and keep your property protected from tear-off through final cleanup, including a magnetic sweep for stray nails.

How long does a siding project take?

A full siding replacement usually takes one to two weeks, depending on the size of the home, the material selected, and the amount of trim, soffit, and fascia detail involved. We keep the job site clean and your home weather-tight throughout the process.

How long does gutter installation take?

Because our seamless gutters are custom-formed on-site, installation is typically completed in a single day for most homes. Gutter guard installation and seasonal cleaning visits are usually same-day as well.

Warranties & Service

Coverage & logistics

What warranties do you offer?

Your project is protected by two layers of warranty. First, the manufacturer's warranty on the materials — often 25 to 50 years, and sometimes lifetime, on quality shingles and siding. Second, our workmanship warranty on the installation itself.

We'll walk you through the exact coverage that applies to your roof, siding, or gutters in your written estimate, so there are no surprises down the road.
